Protecting
yourself
Online
Most people store a lot of personal information on their computers. If you don't protect your computer properly when you're online, it's possible that personal details could be stolen or deleted without your knowledge. Your computer can be attacked in a number of ways over the internet.
How to
Protect yourself
Online
Protecting yourself and your personal information online may seem easy, but it actually not that simple. While the online technology get better by each day, there are more and more people that becomes cybercriminals. Most of the people don't really care about security online and surf the net without cautions. That lead to losing personal information to the cybercriminals without the knowledge. Such as banking information, phone number, passwords, etc.
Tips for
Online
Protection
‣ Keep your computers and mobile devices up to date.
  
‣ Watch out for phishing links and attachments scams.
  
‣ Set strong passwords.
  
‣ Check to see if the site is secure.
  
‣ Use free Wi-Fi with caution.
  
‣ Consider additional protection.
  
‣ Don't overshare on social media.
  
‣ Read the site's privacy policies.
